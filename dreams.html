Although it was intended for others, however, compiling it eventually made me think of my dreams in life too! I figured putting together a related piece could be interesting. This time, my personal ambitions, my dreams and the goals I have for my life are detailed.In glorifying the life of a person and making life hopeful every day the word dream is infinite. Besides that, I love to travel. My top 3 dream places are Maldives, Japan and Dubai. I hope all my dream will be achieved one day, I want to bring my family and friends with me. My dream also want to open cafe bakery beacuse I love baking and coffee so much like they were half of my life.

The first step we need to take to convert our dreams into reality is determination. In many aspects, it will support you. Determination will help us set the course of action and assist us to plan something for the journey. In addition, it will assist us to steady our pace towards a dream. My dream, however big, is that short-term goals will always be saved. This is extremely important because it will not help us get closer to our dream by making hasty decisions. Some dreams take time to nurture, some require procedures to follow, without which our dream can not be accomplished. In life, we may dream big, but we must set long and short-term goals to achieve them. These goals will make us steady, taking a step at a time, without jumping into everything at once in haste. I aspire to become a successful women so that I can do everything and achieve what I want. 🌟
